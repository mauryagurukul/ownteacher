What is homework help, and why do we need it?

To understand what homework help is, we first need to understand what homework is and why it is so critical to our school or college education.

Homework or assignment helps keep the students busy with their learning activities. After the teaching is over, students need to practice it again, and then only they learn and understand better. It helps teachers track the progress of their students. It gives parents a way to analyse how their ward is doing in their academics.

If the student does not do homework, their learning is compromised and hence to force the students to do their homework, every school and college keep a certain percentage of the final grade for the assignment. For example, it may have a weightage of 20%, final exam 50% and midterm exam 30%. By spreading the marks, students focus shifts on all the parts. Just doing well in final exams or midterms won’t help them achieve an A grade. As we can see very clearly in this case, if a student does not pay attention throughout the semester and only focuses on the final exam, the maximum that he can get is 50%! It is a failing mark in most schools and colleges of the USA, Canada, Kuwait, UAE, Saudi Arabia, etc.

If the student also focuses on the midterm, he can get an extra 30% making his grade 80%. It’s still an average grade and not an outstanding one. So it’s very clear from this example that the 20% weightage of homework, even though very low, still forces the students to do their homework diligently. If they skip their homework, then they can get an average grade at best. To get an A grade, they must do their homework regularly, which is the whole purpose of giving it- to keep the students engaged in their studies and learning throughout the semester. Homework help is online tutoring and online homework help service provided by online tutors. There is practically zero face to face homework help these days.

Is homework help ethical?

The short answer is Yes and No.

Homework help is ethical if you take help in your homework with good intent. Learning better and faster is a good intent. In this case, homework help is moral.

If you have a valid reason for taking help in your homework, then also ethical. Some good reasons can be

  • Working part-time to finance your education

  • Teacher giving too much homework
  • Teacher giving complex questions
  • Personal or health issues
  • Need for Faster and more efficient learning
  • Inability to learn at the same pace
  • Missed classes
  • Personal or health issues
Being lazy is evil intent, and in this case, the homework help is unethical.

So we see that the same homework help can be ethical or unethical depending upon the intent with which you seek homework support. Clearly, “Homework help” itself is not moral or evil. It all depends on the purpose and the situation under which you get it done.
